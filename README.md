# Money-Manager-App

## About the App

Money-Manager-App is a personal finance management application designed to help users track, manage, and optimize their spending. Whether you're looking to build better financial habits, track expenses, or plan your budget, this app provides an intuitive and user-friendly interface to take control of your money.

### Key Features

- **Expense Tracking**: Easily log and categorize your daily expenses
- **Budget Management**: Set and monitor budgets for different spending categories
- **Financial Insights**: Visualize your spending patterns with charts and analytics
- **Transaction History**: Keep a detailed record of all your financial transactions
- **Category-Based Organization**: Organize expenses by custom or predefined categories
- **Reports & Analytics**: Generate detailed reports to understand your financial health

### Purpose

This application is built to empower users with better financial awareness and control, making it easier to:
- Monitor where your money goes
- Identify spending patterns
- Set and achieve financial goals
- Make informed financial decisions
